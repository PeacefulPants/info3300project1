Graph 1: Sales by Year for the three most popular console companies

This bar graph visualization offers a numerial analysis of the number of sales (millions of copies shipped) from the three major video game companies: Nintendo, Sony, and Mircosoft by each year.


Graph 2: Break down visualization of Sales by Year for each console company

These scatterplot visualizations offer an in depth understanding of the number of sales (millions of copies shipped) from each video game companies: Nintendo, Sony, and Mircosoft by each year.
The second rows are a series of 2D density plots that show how concentrated most games of the three companies are in their global sales.
